# Walkthrough - Feature Update

## 1. Landing Hub (Home Page)
You now have a central **Command Center** when you log in.
- **Dashboard**: Your classic Habit Tracker.
- **Notes**: A dedicated standalone module for thoughts and reflections.
- **Reminders**: Manage alerts with Google Calendar sync.
- **Still Not Done**: Track missed habits and clear arrears.

## 2. Standalone Notes Module
A beautiful, grid-based notes system:
- **Pin & Color**: Organize notes with custom colors and pin them to the top.
- **Search**: Filter by title or category instantly.
- **Integrated**: Seamlessly linked from the Home page.

## 3. Advanced Reminders & Sync
- **Google Calendar**: Check "Sync to Google Calendar" when creating a reminder to export it directly to your calendar.
- **In-App Alerts**: Custom toast notifications with a **Snooze** feature.

## 4. Still Not Done (Arrears)
- **Catch Up**: View all habits missed in the last 30 days.
- **Acknowledge**: One-click to clear backlog arrears.

## 5. System Quality Audit
- **Reordered Login**: Google sign-in is now prominent and first.
- **UI Polish**: Fixed duplicate buttons and improved layout flow across the app.
- **Bug Fixes**: Resolved critical syntax errors on authentication pages and cleaned up unused code.
